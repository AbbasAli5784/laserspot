Most lasers? Yes. Ours? Not at all. We use the DEKA Motus AX — an advanced Alexandrite (755nm) laser with proprietary Moveo technology, designed from the ground up to be completely pain-free. Here's why it's different: the sapphire-tipped handpiece glides continuously over the skin, gradually heating the follicle instead of zapping it. No snapping, no burning sensation. A built-in skin cooler handles any residual heat. The result is a treatment that works on all skin types (I–VI), including tanned skin and finer hair that other lasers miss — something traditional Alexandrite lasers couldn't do. The 20mm tip also makes sessions up to 50% faster than older systems. Upper lip and chin together take under 5 minutes.
