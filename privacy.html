Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Laser Spot ("we", "us", "our") operates laserspot.ca and its subdomains. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when submitting a consultation request.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit the consultation form on this page, we collect your first name, phone number, and preferred appointment time. We may also automatically collect the URL parameters present when you arrived (e.g. advertising source) for internal marketing analysis only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your name and phone number solely to contact you about scheduling a laser hair removal consultation or treatment at Laser Spot. We do not use your information for any other purpose.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to operate our appointment booking system.

4. Communications

By submitting the form you consent to receiving a phone call or text message from Laser Spot about your requested appointment. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message or by contacting us directly.

5. Data Retention

We retain consultation request information for up to 12 months for the purpose of following up on your inquiry. After that period your information is deleted.

6. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.
